About

What this tool does

RECOVS evaluates whether recoverability can still be established in time under real conditions. If it cannot, continuation is non-admissible and requires restriction, escalation, containment, or halt.

What RECOVS is for

Short-form triage of recoverability conditions across medication, healthcare, finance, housing, identity, and emergency disruption.

What RECOVS is not

It does not replace emergency services, clinicians, lawyers, caseworkers, or operational authorities.

Framework reference: doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19583410
