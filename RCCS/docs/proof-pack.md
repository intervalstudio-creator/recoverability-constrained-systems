# Proof Pack

## Real-world case (validated)
Recoverability Failure in Home Care: Parkinson's Misdiagnosis Risk Detected and Escalated Without Follow-Up

A bedbound patient diagnosed in 2022 received no follow-up until 2026.
A caregiver applied the recoverability condition, detected uncertainty, and triggered escalation.
The GP confirmed absence of diagnostic records.
The patient was referred for reassessment by neurology.

Result:
- non-admissible system detected
- escalation triggered
- continuity corrected
